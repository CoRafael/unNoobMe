Some words about unNoobMe

This website was created for the purposes of the Internet Technolgy assignment on March 2015. Our aim is to find a tutor to people that might need help (noobs) to a particular subject. The time, place and duration are being defined by the person who needs help. In order to motivate user to help, unNoobMe uses points to reward a particular tutor. Someone could use those points for “hiring” a tutor for his/her needs.

How unNoobMe works:

First select the type of lesson that you want (Skype meeting, Library meeting, Home meeting etc).
Let the other user know the subject and topic that you want help with.
Specify the duration of your lesson and choose any date and time that suits you
Wait until another tutor offers his/her services.
Choose your tutor!
